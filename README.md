Bots take up way too much screen space sometimes. This lets you adjust the transparency of their health bars and name tags, or just hide them entirely. You can also set it to automatically hide their HUD when they get killed or hogtied, and even mute their voice lines while they are waiting for rescue so they stop complaining.
