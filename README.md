### [Transparent Bot HUD](https://www.nexusmods.com/warhammer40kdarktide/mods/990)
**The short version:** Lets you adjust the transparency of the bot HUD or hide them completely.

**The details:** Standalone mod that lets you change the transparency of bot health bars and name tags because they take up way too much screen space. You can just hide them entirely if you want. It also lets you set it to automatically hide their HUD when they get killed or hogtied, and you can even mute their voice lines while they are waiting for rescue.
